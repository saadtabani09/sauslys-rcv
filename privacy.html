Sauslys RCV — Privacy Policy

Effective: April 2026 · Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

Sauslys RCV is an internal factory receiving and inventory management tool built by Sauslys Foods, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is designed for use by authorised Sauslys Foods staff only.

Data Collection

The app processes invoice photos using on-device OCR (Tesseract.js). No images or data are uploaded to any external server. All processing happens entirely on your device.

The app stores delivery records, stock data, supplier information, and learned OCR mappings in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it (via Excel download, CSV export, or WhatsApp share).

Camera Access

The app requests camera permission solely to photograph invoices for OCR scanning. Photos are processed locally and are not transmitted, stored on servers, or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or any third-party data collection services. No cookies are set for tracking purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage / IndexedDB). You can clear all data at any time through the app's reset functions or by clearing browser/app data in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
